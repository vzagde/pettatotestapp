About Us

Absolutely nothing compares to the feeling of belongingness one shares with their endearing pet members. We just cannot survive with the daily dose cute, funny, warm and fuzzy in today's big bad world. Pettato is created to help all current pet parents and future pet parents enjoy a harmonious lifestyle that honors your pets natural instincts while bringing them and their humans closer together. The app fosters a sense of community by encouraging pet owners to discuss topics together. The app covers the dynamic aspects of social profile,pet profile,promote your business by creating your business profile,pet adoption with a twist where you can search for pets and people looking for pets, pet mating and dating, lost and found service, ambulance data and cemetery details just so you spend more time cuddling your pet rather than scouring this information from different sources. So hop on to the bandwagon and show your love out loud to the pet fam and enjoy great companionship for life.

Mission -

To enrich your pet's life by providing pawtastic solutions so you can celebrate your caturdays. You want pets, you can pawsitively expand your family because we want to cater to all things animals.

Vision -

It's all about love. To create a petverse (pet universe) and create a community of likeminded pawples and care for our friends just like one of us.
